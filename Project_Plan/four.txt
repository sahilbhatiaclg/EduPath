How They Connect Seamlessly:-

The project follows the MVC (Model-View-Controller) pattern:

The Model (two.md & four.md): Your CSV data and Pandas logic handle the "Truth" of the data.

The View (four.md & three.md): HTML and Matplotlib charts handle how the user "Sees" the data.

The Controller (one.md): Flask acts as the traffic cop, taking a request from the View, asking the Model for data, and sending it back to the View.
